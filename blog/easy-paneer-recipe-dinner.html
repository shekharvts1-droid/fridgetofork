Easy Paneer Bhurji: Your Go-To Paneer Recipe for Dinner!

Craving a delicious yet incredibly simple paneer dish that's perfect for a weeknight dinner? Look no further! This easy paneer bhurji recipe comes together in minutes, packed with flavor, and is guaranteed to satisfy your dinner cravings without any fuss. It’s the ultimate quick and easy paneer recipe for dinner that doesn't compromise on taste.

Gather Your Simple Ingredients for This Easy Paneer Dinner

One of the best parts about this easy paneer recipe for dinner is its straightforward ingredient list. You likely have most of these on hand already!

  • 200g Paneer (Indian cottage cheese), crumbled
  • 1 medium Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium Tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste (or finely minced ginger and garlic)
  • 1-2 Green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red chili powder (optional, for extra heat)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil or Ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Step-by-Step: Cooking Your Easy Paneer Bhurji for Dinner

This paneer dish is famous for its speed and simplicity, making it an ideal easy paneer recipe for dinner. Follow these steps for a delightful meal:

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large pan or kadai, heat the oil or ghee over medium heat.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent, about 3-5 minutes. Then, add the ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies. Sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Stir in the finely chopped tomatoes. Cook until they soften and the oil starts to separate, which usually takes about 5-7 minutes. Now, add the turmeric powder, red chili powder (if using), and salt. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Incorporate Paneer: Gently add the crumbled paneer to the pan. Mix well, ensuring the paneer is evenly coated with the masala. Be careful not to overmix or press too hard.
  5. Finish & Garnish: Cook for just 2-3 minutes, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors without drying out. Stir in the garam masala. Remove from heat, garnish generously with fresh coriander leaves.

Dinner-Time Tips & Variations for Your Easy Paneer Dish

Make this easy paneer recipe for dinner even more special with these simple tweaks and serving suggestions:

  • Add Veggies: For extra nutrition and texture, throw in finely chopped bell peppers or a handful of frozen peas along with the tomatoes.
  • Spice Level: Adjust the green chilies and red chili powder to match your preferred heat level. For a milder version, omit the red chili powder.
  • Serving Suggestions: This quick paneer bhurji is incredibly versatile. Serve it hot with warm rotis, parathas, dinner rolls, or even as a flavorful side with plain rice or pulao. It also makes a fantastic filling for sandwiches or wraps!
  • Fresh Paneer: While store-bought paneer works great, fresh homemade paneer can elevate the taste and texture of this simple paneer dish.
